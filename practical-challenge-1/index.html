Sir Chloe

About

Sir Chloe is a rock band originating in Vermont. Their music may also be classified as garage rock, pop rock, alternative, pop punk, and indie rock. They have been active since 2017.

A few of their most popular songs are Michelle, Animal, and Too Close.

History

In 2017, Dana Foote was a senior at Bennington College studying music theory and composition. In place of a thesis, she created a concert with her friends, marking the beginnings of the band.

Their song "Michelle" went viral on the music and video app TikTok, allowing them to gain wider recognition.

Sir Chloe's earliest known performance was on May 6, 2021, according to concertarchives.org.

"I Am The Dog" is their debut studio album, released in 2023 with the lead single "Hooves."

Fun Facts:

Sir Chloe performed in Phoenix, AZ on August 2023, opening for artists Beck and Phoenix.

According to lead singer Dana Foote, the reason behind the name "Sir Chloe" is because her mother almost named her Chloe, and she was always partial to the name. The prefix "sir" provides an element of androgyny and elegance.

They have a song called "Sedona."
